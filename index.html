RFID Checklist

Arduino prototype that incorporates a MFRC522 RFID reader and an ILI9341 touchscreen to allow users to scan NFC tags attached to their objects. Smart Water Lid

Collaborated with two other students in designing and manufacturing a prototype for a bottle lid that can track water consumption. Key components included the Seeed Studio XIAO nRF52840 and a VL53L4CD Time-of-Flight sensor. Debugged the source code and troubleshot the electronic components of the lid during the prototyping stage.
